ABOUT ME

Currently, I work as a freelance full-stack Shopify developer working with top product brands in the US and handling both the front and backend of their Shopify store. Previously, a web developer, delivering service by building everything from small websites to medium web applications all across the globe.

My professional career is started in 2013 with Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP, and Javascript (LAMP Stack) and went through PHP Frameworks like CodeIgniter, and Laravel as a faster way to build websites and web apps. After that, I wanted to go further in web development so I learned to build a modern Single Page Application with NodeJS and ReactJS.

Since early 2019, I've specialized in Shopify theme development after taking a role in a US-based Shopify Plus partner agency building online stores from the ground up, working with custom codes and template modifications, building Theme Customizer via Schema blocks and sections programming with Shopify Liquid Templating (Ruby) as well as integrating third-party applications and plug-ins and focusing more on developing engaging user experience (UX) and gorgeous user interfaces (UI).

After diving deep into Shopify theme development, I am pursuing to deepen my knowledge in the Shopify ecosystem by developing Shopify Apps utilizing its API to extend Shopify's features and functionalities through the modern technologies I have learned over the years.
